Our Story

From Casablanca to the world SIHA is the app that allows you to get the best products in your city from your home.

SIHA is a Moroccan quick-commerce start-up founded in Casablanca in 2020. It is an on-demand courier service that purchases, picks up, and delivers products ordered through its mobile app. It aspires to be a multi-category lifestyle app with food delivery being the most popular offering. In fact, The company has a vision to be a lifestyle app with all urban services available easily through its smartphone application. Food delivery service remains its most popular service. Other services available on the app include Groceries, Pharmacy, ... The food business allows users to find and place orders with their favourite restaurants which is picked up when ready and delivered to the user's doorstep.
